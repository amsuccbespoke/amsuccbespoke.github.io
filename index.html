About FAWLUX BESPOKE

Founded by Abdulkareem Fawaz, the visionary behind FAWLUX (formerly AMSUCC BESPOKE), we are a Lagos-based menswear brand driven by precision, artistry, and timeless style. From regal nativewear to refined English tailoring, every piece embodies excellence in fit, fabric, and finishing — defined by clean lines, lasting craftsmanship, and understated elegance. Every stitch is intentional — because you deserve nothing less.
