About AMSUCC BESPOKE

Founded by ABDULKAREEM FAWAZ, the visionary behind AMSUCC BESPOKE, we are a Lagos-based menswear brand dedicated to excellence in fit, fabric, and finishing—offering everything from regal nativewear to sharp English tailoring, all defined by clean lines, durable construction, and elegant details, with every stitch intentional because you deserve nothing less.
